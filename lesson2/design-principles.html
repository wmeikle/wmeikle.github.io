Design Principles

Repetition

Repetition is a key design principle for this site. It is in every part, whether on the front page, or in a subreddit it is a prevelent feature. It helps users navigate, and keeps all information displayed organized.

Proximity

Proximity becomes a very important design element for a company such as Nike. When there are so many different products offered by a single site, proximity helps users to understand which products go together, and where they can search for more of a specific product. It keeps what could be a massive amount of confusion through chaotic design schemes, and pushes it to become a site that is easy on the eyes, and just as easy to navigate.

Contrast

Contrast helps keep the attention of users where the site is trying to display something important. Nintendo does this in a big way, highlighting new releases, and keeping other information in smaller organized blocks. This very successfully creates a focal point on the newest products.
